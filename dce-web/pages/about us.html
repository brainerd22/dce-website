About us

Welcome to Deliverance Church International - Embakasi. We are glad that you found time to visit our website. Our prayer is that your time with us here will give you an opportunity to learn more about us - Our Vision, Mission, Values and believes. Our Mission is disciple believers to live true to God's word. You are welcome to join our church and connect with a family of people that are passionate to know and serve God. Deliverance Church Embakasi was founded in 2004 by Rev. Elias Gitonga. We are located Along the Eastern Bypass, between East Africa School of Aviation (EASA) and Tuskys Supermarket in Embakasi - Nairobi, Kenya. Our desire is to encourage everyone, share the bibical message that Jesus Christ is the risen Son of God. He loves each person unconditionally and that He desires a personal one-on-one relationship with everyone! Enjoy your time on our website and remember, we shall be more that glad to see you join us for service this week. Stay blessed
