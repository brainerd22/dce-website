Hello and Welcome

Welcome to DCEmbakasi website. Here you will find useful information, access resources, and connect with the larger DCEmbakasi family. We desire to help you grow in your walk with God. As our lives are being transformed by the saving knowledge of Christ, we in turn transform our families and the communities in which we live. As much as we value personal spiritual growth, we also empower the youth, women, men and also families in small group meetings. Our aim as a church is to be an all round kind of church, where we handle spiritual matters and also physical and psychological that arise with the help of our pastors and leaders.
